# Whetstone — Consumption Log

Append-only. One line per consumed source that didn't earn a concept — a searchable trace you
can promote to a concept later. The /whetstone skill appends here; nothing rewrites this file.

Format: `- <date> [<kind>] <title> · why: <one line> · <url> · <#tags>`
